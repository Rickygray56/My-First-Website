About Me

My name is Richard Gray II and I'm a full stack javascript developer. I am 25 years old and I have a passion for all things technology. I wish to be able to establish a career in computer science to build upon my technical skillset.

My Background

My background in technology stems back to high school where I took 2 courses in computer programming primarily learning the Java language. I then switched to gears to Audio Productions once I attended college to become an audio engineer. I enjoyed my time in school for music, but realized it was more of a hobby for me. Once I determined computer programming was a career I wanted to pursue, I enrolled into a coding bootcamp called CoderCamps. During my time I learned how to program using Javascript, MEAN stack including REACT and REDUX, and backend foundations using MySQL, NoSQL, and Node.Js.

Why I decided to learn programming

I have always been fascinated by the world of technology. More than anything, I love the art of creation. I enjoy the process of creating things and taking something from scratch and transforming it into a fully polished masterpiece. With this in mind, I started my search for a career that would allow me to create. I then came across computer programming which prompted me to try free coding websites like khan academy and codecademy and I was hooked ever since.
